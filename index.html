Scientific Interests

During my studies in the Physics Department, I developed a passion for astronomy, captivated by the vastness of the universe and its mysteries. However, as I approached graduation, my interest in computers and programming grew. I discovered my knack for using problem-solving skills to create and overcome challenges, and the ever-evolving Information Technology field offered endless opportunities to learn and grow. I believe my astronomy background will be an asset in my new IT career. My knowledge of physics and mathematics will provide a solid foundation for learning new programming languages and technologies, while my research and data analysis experience will be valuable in my new role.
